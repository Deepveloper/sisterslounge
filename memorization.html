An online Qur'an study and memorization platform designed exclusively for sisters by the sisters

Quran Memorization Program

Objectives

This is student specific. It will support the student at all levels until they are able to memorize a good portion of the Qur'an, if not the entire portion of the Qur'an

All students will be attached to specific Qur'an mentors who will support them individually, recite unto them and listen to their recitations

The classes hold three times a week and every student will be alloted 15 minutes according to their attendance
