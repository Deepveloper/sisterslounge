Tilawa Program

Objectives

This course will provide the students all foundamental knowledge that will enable them recite the Qur'an comfortably by learning:

    All the Arabic alphabets and their places of articulation
    All Arabic vowel sounds
    Identification of alphabets with changing position
    Arabic Handwritting
    Fundamentals of Tajweed
    and others

This course is designed for students who can not recite the Glorious Qur'an
